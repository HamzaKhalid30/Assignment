## Findings and Details

### 1. Ransomware Attack on Hospitals
- Hospitals targeted by ransomware.
- Systems locked, affecting patient care.

### 2. Data Breach in Social Media Platform
- Millions of user records leaked.
- Caused by weak security controls.

### 3. Phishing Attack on Banking Users
- Fake emails used to steal login credentials.
- Users redirected to malicious websites.

### 4. Supply Chain Attack
- Attackers compromised third-party software.
- Affected multiple organizations.

### 5. Cloud Misconfiguration Breach
- Sensitive data exposed due to misconfigured cloud storage.
- No proper access control applied.

## Reflection

This activity helped me understand real-world cyber attacks and their impact. It showed that most incidents occur due to basic security mistakes, highlighting the importance of strong security practices.
