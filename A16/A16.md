# A16: Discover 10 Different Types of Locks in Use

## Overview

Locks are physical security mechanisms used to protect assets and restrict unauthorized access. Different types of locks provide varying levels of security depending on their design and usage.

## Findings and Details

1. **Padlock**: Portable lock used for gates and lockers.
2. **Deadbolt Lock**: Strong lock used in doors for high security.
3. **Knob Lock**: Common in residential doors.
4. **Lever Handle Lock**: Used in commercial buildings.
5. **Smart Lock**: Controlled via mobile app or PIN.
6. **Biometric Lock**: Uses fingerprint or face recognition.
7. **Chain Lock**: Adds extra layer of door security.
8. **Cam Lock**: Used in cabinets and drawers.
9. **Mortise Lock**: Strong lock embedded inside doors.
10. **Electronic Keycard Lock**: Used in hotels and offices.

## Reflection

This activity helped me understand the importance of physical security. Even with advanced cybersecurity, physical access can compromise systems, making locks an essential part of security.
