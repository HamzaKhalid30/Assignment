# A10: Discover Privacy Technique Used Offline

## Overview

While exploring offline privacy techniques, I identified that screen lock and device authentication (password, PIN, biometric) are commonly used methods to protect personal data. These techniques ensure that only authorized users can access the device and its stored information.

## Practical

1. Open your device settings (mobile or laptop).
2. Navigate to Security / Lock Screen settings.
3. Enable any lock method:
   - PIN
   - Password
   - Fingerprint / Face lock

## Findings and Details

- I discovered that offline privacy is maintained through authentication mechanisms, which restrict unauthorized access to devices.
- Techniques such as PIN, password, and biometrics ensure that only verified users can access sensitive data.
- These methods protect data at rest, including files, photos, and personal information stored on the device.
- Even if a device is lost or stolen, these privacy controls prevent unauthorized access.
- Offline privacy techniques are essential because physical access to a device can otherwise lead to complete data compromise.

## Reflection

This activity helped me understand that privacy is not only important online but also offline. Simple measures like screen locks and authentication can protect sensitive personal data. It highlights that strong access control is essential to maintain privacy even without internet connectivity.
